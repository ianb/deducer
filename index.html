This is a logic game. A board is laid out with specific choices (like B). But to start you don't know what those choices are! You have to deduce what goes where.

To help you, you can acquire some rules. Hit the button and you'll get a bunch of rules or hints about how the board is laid out. These rules take a few forms:

  1. One tile is to the left of another tile, like:
    C ... 2

    This means that the C is somewhere to the left of 2.
  2. One tile is next to another, like:
    1 u

    This means that 1 is next to u — but their order could be reversed (i.e., it will be u then 1).
  3. And other stuff.
